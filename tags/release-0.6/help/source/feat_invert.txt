= Invert =
Inverts the colors in the image.

|| image:feat_desaturate_color.png || image:feat_invert.png ||

== Method ==
Invert is only available from the Python interpreter.

*Free functions*
|| invert() || Inverts the active image ||
|| invert(i1, i2, ...) || Inverts the specified images ||

*Image methods*
|| image.invert() || Inverts the specified image ||
