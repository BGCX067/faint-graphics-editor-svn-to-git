= Snap to object points =
While drawing or moving a point, the adjusted point can be snapped to
certain points in objects.

||\image(feat_snap_object_points.png)||A line with end-points snapped to the center of a circle\br and the middle of the left side of a rectangle||

To snap, hold \image(key_ctrl.png) while moving a point or drawing shapes.
