= The grid control =
The grid control enables and disables the snappable \ref(feat_grid.txt,Grid) and adjusts the grid-spacing.

|| image:gui_grid_control_disabled.png || image:gui_grid_control_enabled.png ||
|| When disabled, the control is collapsed. || When enabled, the spacing can be adjusted ||

To enable the grid for an image, click the grid button. This reveals
the grid spacing controls.

|| image:icon_info.png || The spacing digit can be dragged for quick-adjustment: ||
